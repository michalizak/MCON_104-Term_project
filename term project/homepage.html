About Us

Shoe Stop was created by a young girl with a dream. Michelle, the dreamer, a teacher by trade, had a creative side that very few people knew about. She dreamt of finding an outlet for her creativity, while still being able to help her students fulfill their own dreams.

Armed with a love of business and a passion for style, the teacher became a student. She found herself a factory and a customer, and in 2015, Shoe Stop Inc. was born. She works tirelessly every season, personally picking patterns and styles,and traveling overseas to ensure that her fabrics are just right

You can Shop on Shoe Stop from the comfort of your own home, anywhere.
